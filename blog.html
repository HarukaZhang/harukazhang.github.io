ZHANG JunYao

lalaethzhang@gmail.com

Junyao ZHANG is a passionate game developer and creative filmmaker, focusing on video game cinematic design, storytelling, and exploring audiovisual language. Her work explores complex, dark themes, with an emphasis on the relationship between modern technology and humanity. Her projects often combine elements of soft science fiction and low magic, aiming to convey emotions and philosophical reflections.

Her creative endeavors primarily examine the boundaries between the living and the inanimate, the human and the non-human. Through game design and film scriptwriting, he explores the conflicts between technology and human existence, as well as questions of moral choices and existential crises in a future society.

Junyao ZHANG's work has been showcased at various international exhibitions and recognized across multiple media platforms. Key projects include video games that explore philosophy, emotions, and ethics, as well as film scripts that present profound artistic expressions.

She graduated from EUR ArTeC and is currently a core member of the cinematic team at Ubisoft Chengdu studio, where she is committed to advancing cross-disciplinary innovation between game and film arts.
