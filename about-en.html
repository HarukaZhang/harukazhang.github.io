Zhang Junyao is a creative director specializing in visual storytelling and game cinematic design, with a dual background in film and digital arts. She excels at constructing real-time narrative scenes imbued with emotional tension and immersion. Her work integrates dramaturgy, audiovisual aesthetics, and AAA console game logic, focusing on the deep coordination of camera language, character performance, and gameplay progression.

She has years of experience in independent short film production and has conducted research and creative work in interactive media and digital storytelling within the French national key project EUR ArTeC. She currently works at Ubisoft Chengdu Studio, where she has contributed to and led the cinematic design and implementation of multiple sequences in Assassin's Creed: Shadow and other unannounced projects.

She is familiar with the game narrative production pipeline, proficient in using Ubisoft's proprietary engine for real-time performance, and capable of efficient collaboration with narrative, quest, level design, art, animation, VFX, sound, and technical teams, driving scripts from concept to full implementation.
