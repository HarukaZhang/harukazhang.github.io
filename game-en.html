Rengakai – First Confrontation with Ukita Naoie

//For the full mission flow in English, click here to view player-recorded footage on Youtube //

//The "Nobles" questline is one of the key preview missions for AC: Shadow. Some Rengakai shots were also included in the global trailer //


Prerequisite: Players are invited by Daimyo Kuroda Kanbei to attend a Rengakai (a traditional Japanese poetry assembly) at Himeji Castle.

Hachirō (Hideie) guides the player to their seat, triggering the scene.

Characters: Player (selectable Naoe/Yasuke), Ukita Naoie ("Noble"), Kuroda Kanbei, Konishi Yukinaga, Renga Poet, Calligrapher, Other Guests ×3, Ronin ×2, Servants

Performance Narrative: Guests exchange Renga poems to mask probing and hidden tensions; calm on the surface, turbulent beneath. Daimyo Ukita Naoie initiates conversation with the player, testing them repeatedly, and then showcases his ambition through Renga, challenging Kuroda Kanbei, bringing the room to a sudden hush. As the atmosphere tightens, a group of ronin storms into the courtyard, firing indiscriminately into the assembly. Guests scatter, and the player and Ukita Naoie rise to face the attacking ronin together, before the game returns to the main mission flow.

Repelling the Ronin – Escalating Tensions and Gathering Clues

//For the full mission flow in English, click here to view player-recorded footage on Youtube //


Prerequisite: Triggered after the player and Naoie jointly repel the attacking ronin.

Characters: Naoe, Yasuke, Ukita Naoie ("Noble"), Kuroda Kanbei, Konishi Yukinaga, Castle Commander, Kuroda Retainers ×3, Ukita Retainers ×2

Performance Narrative: After the battle, the courtyard is in disarray, and Naoe and Yasuke regroup. Naoie angrily informs Kanbei that his son Hachirō (Hideie) has been captured by the ronin, demanding full alert and deployment of his troops across Himeji Castle. Once Naoie departs, Naoe, Yasuke, and Kanbei cooperate to organize the clues they currently have: the missing Torbei and the gunpowder.

Spring and Autumn – The Death of the "Noble"

//For the full mission flow, click here to view player-recorded footage on Youtube //


Prerequisite: Triggered when the player infiltrates Himeji Castle's main keep and defeats Ukita Naoie in the boss battle.

Characters: Naoe, Yasuke, Ukita Naoie ("Noble")

Performance Narrative: A pivotal moment in the "Spring and Autumn" chapter, marking the end of the "Noble" Ukita Naoie's fate amidst sudden storms and chaos.

Although Naoie joined the "New Shogunate," his motive was not solely power, but also to protect his son Hachirō. Players can choose whether to spare him; if spared, they receive his poem for Hachirō. Regardless, Naoie's death is inevitable.

Raid Plan

//For the full mission flow, click here to view player-recorded footage on Youtube //


Prerequisite: Triggered when the player prepares all necessary arrangements and meets Nakamura Tadashige in the downtown area of Miki Castle.

Characters: Naoe, Yasuke, Nakamura Tadashige

Performance Narrative: The three consolidate the current clues and military resources, jointly devising the assault plan against the "Ox." Yasuke, alongside ally Koshiro, directly confronts the group to draw fire, while Naoe infiltrates Miki Castle with Nakamura to rescue the captured hostages.

Broken Horn – The Death of the "Ox"

//For the full mission flow, click here to view player-recorded footage on Youtube //


Prerequisite: After Yasuke kills the "Horn" in battle and discovers it was actually a young monk, the player infiltrates Miki Castle's main keep and defeats Bessho Harumasa in the boss battle to trigger the scene.

Characters: Naoe, Yasuke, Bessho Harumasa ("Ox")

Performance Narrative: Bessho Harumasa is struck down and kneels; Naoe and Yasuke regroup. Naoe questions Bessho about the box taken by the "New Shogunate," but Bessho claims ignorance. Frustrated, Naoe hears Bessho mock their naive and hypocritical sense of justice. Yasuke ends Bessho's life in the way of a samurai, yet remains tormented by guilt and doubt over killing the "Horn." Naoe comforts Yasuke. The game then returns to the main flow.

Caro Reveal

//This is Yasuke's final mission. I haven't reached this part myself yet, so I'm temporarily using a online-recorded video /(ㄒoㄒ)/~~ //

//Caro's appearance footage was edited into the Assassin's Creed: Shadow global story trailer //


Prerequisite: To obtain clues about his enemesis Caro, Yasuke meets the "Silver Queen" and follows her indoors to trigger the scene.

Characters: Yasuke, Hosokawa Tama ("Silver Queen"), Nuno Caro

Performance Narrative: The "Silver Queen" serves matcha to Yasuke. As he drinks, he asks about Caro. The "Silver Queen" reveals her true identity as Hosokawa Tama (daughter of the traitorous Oda retainer Akechi Mitsuhide) and tests Yasuke's abilities and his hatred toward Caro. Yasuke tries to ask more, but discovers the tea is drugged. Dazed, Nuno Caro suddenly appears; Tama is revealed to be aligned with him. Caro does not recognize Yasuke and mocks him, while Tama declares she will personally kill Yasuke to avenge her father. Caro leaves Yasuke in Tama's hands. Yasuke loses consciousness…

Yasuke Free

//This is Yasuke's final mission. I haven't reached this part myself yet, so I'm temporarily using a player-recorded video /(ㄒoㄒ)/~~ //


Prerequisite: Immediately following the previous scene, Caro hands Yasuke over to Hosokawa Tama, and Yasuke loses consciousness.

Characters: Yasuke, Hosokawa Tama

Performance Narrative: Yasuke awakens in a warehouse, only to be held at gunpoint by Tama. The two confront each other—Yasuke is furious at Tama's deception, while she explains that after Honnoji, she and her brother were under Caro's control. To escape Caro's influence, she demands that Yasuke rescue her brother, promising to spare him if he complies. They reach an agreement, Tama lets Yasuke go, and the game returns to its main flow.

Lakeside Confession – Naoe x Koshiro

//For the full mission walkthrough, click here to view the player-recorded video on Youtube //


Prerequisite: Koshiro, unable to continue fighting, wants to say goodbye. Naoe encourages him by helping him retrieve the box from his past woodworking. They walk and talk along Lovers' Lake, reaching the lake's central island, where their feelings deepen. Koshiro sits under a cherry tree, and the scene triggers when the player sits as well.

Characters: Naoe, Koshiro

Performance Narrative: A relatively subtle Eastern-style confession scene. Koshiro still wants to leave, and Naoe tries to stop him. Depending on the affection level and player choices, three branches are possible: Love / Friendship / Rejection:

- Love Branch: Affection meets the condition and the player selects "For example, me." Koshiro gives Naoe a hairpin, Naoe steals a kiss, and they confirm their feelings, sealing their bond with a lakeside kiss.

- Friendship Branch: Affection meets the condition and the player selects "It's up to you." Koshiro gives Naoe a small wooden sword, and they agree to be allies.

- Rejection Branch: Affection does not meet the condition and the player selects "For example, me." Koshiro is shocked, Naoe responds awkwardly. Koshiro gives Naoe a small wooden sword, and they agree to be allies.
