Interactive Short Film
Her Room - Omicron 30mins 2022
Director · Screenwriter · Editor

Omicron enters a temporal rift composed of the memories of a human woman.
Time interweaves between 1995 and 2040, as chaotic memories and apocalyptic truths gradually emerge.
Viewers can make choices as Omicron, guiding the world toward different futures.

/* This work is part of the EUR ArTeC master's graduation project “Exploring Temporal Possibilities in Interactive Film”
Exhibited on October 5–6, 2022 at the Cité Internationale des Arts, France /

Click here to view the thesis

/ EUR ArTeC (École Universitaire de Recherche ArTeC) is a top-tier research institute for art and technology, jointly founded by University of Paris 8 and University of Paris 10. It is funded by the French National Research Agency (ANR) and is a national key project under the French Future Investment Program (PIA). The institute focuses on research and creation at the intersection of art, technology, and digital media, aiming to cultivate a new generation of dual-skilled talents in “scientific research – artistic creation.” */

Interactive Video Short
Room 1 – La Chambre 1 30 mins 2021
Director · Screenwriter · Editor · Music

After being trapped alone in a bedroom for 408 days, a girl suddenly appears and leads me out…

This video is an interactive experience exploring resilience, resistance, and true freedom during XX period. Is enduring everything resilience? Is escaping reality a form of resistance? When we are confined, where does freedom lie?

/\* This work is part of the laboratory workshop “Digital Narratives as Civic Expression” led by Michel Meimaris, Arnaud Laborderie, and Evika Karamagioli, organized in collaboration with EUR ArTeC and the University of Athens, aiming to cultivate students' ability to use narrative as a personal and civic form of expression. \*/
